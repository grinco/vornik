Healing candidates

candidate · trial · promotion

A healing candidate is a structural repair proposed against a workflow that regressed — a retry-budget tweak, a verifier step, or an architect proposal. Nothing here changes production on its own. Each candidate must clear a trial (a non-production replay or static validation) and then be promoted by an operator. Open a candidate to see the evidence, the expected effect, and the trial scorecard before you decide whether to trust the repair.
